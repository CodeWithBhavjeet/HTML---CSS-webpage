Support Facilities

How are you feeling today? Click on the options below to see some suggestions for improving your mental health.

I feel stressed

It's natural to feel stressed sometimes. Take a few deep breaths, step away for a moment, or try a short walk. Activities like listening to calm music or journaling can help reduce stress.

I feel anxious

Anxiety can feel overwhelming. Try grounding exercises like the 5-4-3-2-1 method (name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you can smell, and 1 you taste). You’re not alone in this journey.

I feel sad

Feeling sad is okay. It’s important to acknowledge your emotions. Talk to a trusted friend, family member, or seek professional support. Doing something creative or spending time in nature can also lift your mood.

I feel happy

That’s wonderful! Celebrate your happiness and keep doing activities that bring you joy. Share your positivity with others around you!

I feel overwhelmed

When feeling overwhelmed, break tasks into smaller, manageable steps. Take time to pause and prioritize self-care. Remember, it’s okay to ask for help when needed.
