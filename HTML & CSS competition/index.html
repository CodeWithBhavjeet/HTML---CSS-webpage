Leveraging Innovation to Break the Stigma

Technology offers endless possibilities to improve mental health awareness. From AI-powered chatbots providing support to meditation apps promoting mindfulness, we are in a digital era where solutions are just a tap away.

Take short breaks during work to relax your mind.

Practice mindfulness and deep breathing exercises daily.

Reach out to friends or family when feeling stressed.

Limit screen time and engage in physical activities.

Use mental health apps to track your mood and habits.
